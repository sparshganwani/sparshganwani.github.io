About My Journey

From finance to product management, it has been a volatile journey in figuring my interests (my resume is hated by HRs xD). Nevery found myself a fit in core finance role, solving problems, building for user delight and wearing multiple hats always interested me - and here I am. A backpacker by heart, athlete since childhood and geek for nutrition and exercise science, always open to interesting conversations around it.
